Run-Down

The people who have had a bad experience have a much larger chance of being a patient who has spent a long time going through the system and has either a chronic illness or a supporter of someone with a chronic illness. However, in saying this, many people who just go through the system for short periods of time will still occasionally have a bad experience as well. People who have a bad experience with the medical system almost always experience one or more of the few main issues being, misdiagnoses, exploitation of patients, being treated badly causing physical damage, being treated badly causing mental damage, lack of funding meaning patients are not able to get correct treatment or they will just have a bad experience in general from other factors.

Heartfelt advice from people who have spent most of there lifes in the system

My advice for others would be to make sure you speak up, if you feel something isn't right.

About / Contact

This website is about teaching people who are struggling and going through the medical system and teach them about the issues it has, how it may affect them and how to overcome it. It will provide you with an in-sight into the issues and give some advice on how to overcome them.
